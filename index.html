Welcome to Danish Alpsankhayak Shikshan Evam Prashikshan Sansthan

Danish Alpsankhayak Shikshan Evam Prashikshan Sansthan, Sultanpur, has a well-planned campus of its own sprawling land with well-groomed green lawns, pampered flower beds and trees. Its location just outside the town keeps it pollution free, quiet and serene yet accessible from the city. The institute has all the modern resources to impart quality education and facilitate better learning.

Our College is conducting BTC & B.Ed courses under National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).
